nxmctree.history module¶

Functions related to histories on trees.

Every node in a Markov chain tree history has a known state.

nxmctree.history.get_history_lhood(T, edge_to_P, root, root_prior_distn, node_to_state)[source]¶

Compute the probability of a single specific history.

nxmctree.history.get_history_feas(T, edge_to_A, root, root_prior_fset, node_to_state)[source]¶

Compute the feasibility of a single specific history.

nxmctree.history.gen_plausible_histories(node_to_data_fset)[source]¶

Yield histories compatible with directly observed data.

Each history is a map from node to state. Some of these histories may have zero probability when the shape of the tree and the structure of the transition matrices is taken into account.

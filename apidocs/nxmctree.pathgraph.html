nxmctree.pathgraph module¶

Algorithms specialized to homogeneous hidden state paths, for testing.

All algorithms assume that the history is not branching and that the hidden state transition matrix is the same for each neighboring pair of time points. The implementation may also assume feasibility of all paths. These algorithms are from Biological Sequence Analysis by Durbin et al.
